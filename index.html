About
Abiola

After graduating in 2017 with a Bachelor’s Degree (Second Class Upper), I started learning how to write code by enrolling in online courses, and since then, I have worked on several projects: web pages, database structures, creating and consuming APIs, client and server-side validations, and deployment of projects. I use JavaScript, Python, Ruby, React, Redux, Vue, Python, Flask, Django, and Ruby on Rails to build software solutions for small and medium-scale businesses.

In the past 3 years, I have worked independently and as part of a team building software solutions using different technologies, crafting thoughtful and inclusive experiences that adhere to web standards. I acquired project and time management skills, as well as the ability to communicate with team members and clients while effectively meeting milestones and deadlines.

Before now, I worked at Ustacky as a Full-Stack developer and worked on a web service that revolutionizes the way candidates find jobs and how employers hire staff. I also mentored intern developers on the use of Git, Flask, and Vue through over 30 virtual sessions.

I've spent the last 10 months completing coursework at Microverse, a remote software development that allows devs worldwide to pair-program and build projects together. I mastered algorithms, and data structure, developing skills in remote pair-programming using GitHub, industry-standard gitflow, and daily standups to communicate and collaborate with international remote developers.

I am currently working as a freelance web developer. I'm open to job offer and also available to discuss your project over the email, Skype or on any of my social media. These days I enjoy spending my free time playing video games, football, and watching movies.
